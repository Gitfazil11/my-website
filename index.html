About our Innovation

Multi-Sensor Fusion with Edge AI:

Combines camera, thermal, acoustic, and vibration sensors, processed locally on Raspberry Pi/Jetson Nano for accurate elephant detection in all conditions.

Confidence-Based Decision Engine :

Uses a scoring system to trigger tiered responses (log, LED, buzzer) ensuring fewer false alarms and smarter alerts.

Local + Long-Range Alerts:

Immediate siren/LED alerts for villagers and LoRa-based messages for rangers, working even without mobile networks or internet.

Scalable & Predictive Design:

Modular setup allows easy sensor addition, with future upgrades like cloud dashboards, predictive elephant path mapping, and drone surveillance.
